Disk Assignment

To assign hard disk :
lsdev | grep hdisk
lspv -size
lsmap -all
mkvg -f -vg rootvg_RHELlpar hdisk1
mklv -lv RHELlpar_client rootvg_RHELlpar 745G
mkvdev -vdev RHELlpar_client -vadapter vhost0
